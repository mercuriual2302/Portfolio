    I'm a Technical Support Engineer at Beckhoff Automation UK, based at Alderley Park. Day to day I handle first-line support across TwinCAT 3 PLC, TwinCAT HMI, EtherCAT, and Beckhoff IPCs, as well as building demo projects and proof-of-concepts for customers. I've also co-delivered TwinCAT PLC and HMI training courses and done on-site consultancy visits to customers including Mercedes AMG High Performance Powertrains. Outside of that I've been building a full PLC and HMI solution on the CX9240 running Beckhoff RT Linux, which also led me to write a desktop tool that automates the whole IPC provisioning workflow over SSH. It's been a good way to keep the software side sharp while going deep on the automation stack. I studied Computer Science at the University of Manchester (BSc Hons, 2:1), where I worked on things like a Hex AI agent, Formula Student autonomous racing, and a full-stack events platform for the CS society. On the side I've worked as a team leader at Manchester United and Stockport County FC, which, if nothing else, teaches you to stay calm under pressure. I'm also a keen footballer and have played at district and international level. Outside of work I'm passionate about motorsport, following both F1 and endurance racing.
