    I’m Anurag Mathew, a recent Computer Science graduate from the University of Manchester (BSc Hons, 2:1) with hands-on experience across software development, AI, and web technologies. I’m passionate about solving complex problems through code and have worked on a range of impactful projects — from building AI agents that compete in strategic games like Hex, to developing full-stack web applications with REST APIs, MySQL, and MongoDB. Currently, I work as a Technical Support Engineer at Beckhoff Automation, where I provide first-line technical support across TwinCAT 3 PLC/HMI, EtherCAT, and Beckhoff IPCs. I also build demo projects and proof-of-concepts that showcase machine learning and computer vision integrations for industrial automation. This role has deepened my understanding of embedded systems, real-time control, and customer-focused engineering. My journey also includes contributing to Formula Student AI, developing algorithms and simulation tools for autonomous racing systems, and collaborating with the UniCS Dev Team to build and maintain internal web infrastructure. Whether I’m coding or on the pitch, I bring energy, precision, and a team-first mindset to everything I do.
